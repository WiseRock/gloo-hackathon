WiseConnect

Team Wise Rock has developed an AI app that creates interactive, role-play scenarios from educational and spiritual content. Our coach and avatar smooth the transition between knowledge and application in response to the challenge of Equipping the Church. This technology matters to everyone who publishes educational and motivational content but longs to see more engagement and application.
