Wise Connect

Wise Rock"s team has developed an AI app, Wise Connect, that provides interactive conversations with life-like AI powered human avatars that help people move from head knowledge about evangelism to confident practical experience talking to others about Jesus. This same interactive training platform can be applied to many other purposes such as conflict resolution, sales training, or interviewing. This technology matters to everyone who publishes educational and motivational content but longs to see more people move from consumption to real-life application.
